Methodology & Assumptions

Compounding: Discrete at selected frequency. Period rate: (1 + net_annual)^(1/n) − 1.

Net rate: CAGR − Management Fee.

Contributions: Start or end of period. Reduced by transaction fee. Annual growth applied.

Inflation: Real = nominal ÷ (1 + infl)^years.

Stress: Drawdown applied at start of chosen year.

Tax: End-of-horizon or annual simplified model.

Monte Carlo: Box-Muller normal sampling, N(μ, σ/√n). P10/P50/P90 bands.

Worst year: CAGR − 2×Vol heuristic.

Double: Rule of 72.

Live Off Your Portfolio: For each year of accumulation, a binary search finds the maximum initial monthly withdrawal (increasing with inflation each year) such that the portfolio lasts the entire withdrawal horizon. Uses the same net return (CAGR − fees) and inflation assumptions. "Real" values are deflated by cumulative inflation to that year.
